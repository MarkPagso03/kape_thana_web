How to get there?

Located in Abagatanen, Macaboboni Agno, Pangasinan, Agno, Philippines,
traveling to Kape - Thana Beach House is easy and enjoyable.
The journey is just 3 - 4 hours from Urdaneta City Pangasinan.

From Urdaneta City, head towards the Urdaneta Junction and get onto the
MacArthur Highway (National Highway) heading west toward Dagupan City.
Drive along the Urdaneta-Dagupan Road or via Pangasinan-La Union Highway and continue heading west.
Pass through key towns such as Santa Barbara, Calasiao, and Dagupan City.
At Dagupan, merge onto the road leading to Lingayen, then continue west toward Alaminos City.
After reaching Alaminos, follow signs for Bani/Agno.
Take the Alaminos-Bani Road and drive southward toward Agno.
Along the way, you'll pass through the town of Bani.
Once in Agno, follow local signs to your destination,
whether it’s a beach resort, municipal hall, or another point of interest.
